About Me

I am a PhD candidate at the University of Hong Kong, specializing in high energy astrophysicsal phenomena. My research focuses on tidal disruption events (TDEs), cataclysmic occurrences where stars are torn apart by black holes, and their role as powerful probes of black hole populations, early stellar systems, and galactic structures.
